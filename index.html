The Shred Creative Design Process

How We Work With You
1

Set up a meeting

Whether it’s a Skype chat, a drop into the studio or we come to you we always prefer to have a decent chat before we start a project. We’re experts at getting under the skin of a project and this not only saves time but helps us push the limits of the project to exceed your expectations.

2

Work the Concept

With most projects there’ll be some time for creative concepts, this is where the whole studio gets around the desk and kicks the project about. It’s time well spent, we get loads of ideas out there in a very short amount of time. We find this is where the magic happens.

3

Quote and Proceed

Once we have a clear idea what the project will entail we set up a quote. If you have given us a budget then we’ll have done everything we can to leave something in the pot, either way you’ll know what it’s going to cost before we begin work. Once you’re happy we’ll proceed with the work.

4

Assign and Create

Once we’ve done the magic bit your project is assigned to a designer and a member of support team. They’ll keep you in the loop of where your project is at. If you’d like to jump in on the process as we design your job then we’re happy to do this by appointment.

Skills and Services

And We think you’ll love our Work

Design

Graphic Design is what we do, stunning design for anything from a simple logo to a full brochure or website. Our team here in our Plymouth studio can help with your next project.

Print

We’re a full service creative agency so we can handle all of your print requirements, flyers, brochures, catalogues and business cards and even less day to day print like packaging.

Copywriting

We started our business on the back of a great little bike magazine. We know how to write captivating copy and engage with your audience, whether it’s for a brochure or for your Facebook updates.

Web Design

We create websites using WordPress or dedicated bespoke sites. All of our sites offer great levels of interaction with social media and are super easy to update by your team.

Retail

We’re Shopify Experts, perfect for online retail, we’re Vend Partners so we can help you set up your over the counter point of sale and we’re also pretty good at innovative solutions to help manage your stock and sales.

Mailchimp

As MailChimp experts we will guide through creating engaging email, well designed and written to be read, in a way which captures the readers attention and gets your news across.
